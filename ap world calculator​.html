About This AP World Calculator

This AP World calculator is designed for students who want a fast and flexible way to estimate their AP World History exam result. It blends multiple-choice and written responses into a weighted composite, then maps that composite to a projected AP score using the compression level you choose.

  • Strict compression uses tougher AP score thresholds.
  • Standard compression keeps thresholds in a typical middle range.
  • Lenient compression lowers thresholds to model a more favorable scoring curve.
  • Optimization bias lets you emphasize multiple-choice or writing strength.

Because AP scoring can vary by administration, this tool is best used as an estimator rather than an official predictor.
