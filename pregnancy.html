Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is a pregnancy conception calculator?

It provides an estimate based on standard pregnancy dating assumptions. Actual ovulation and conception can vary, especially with irregular cycles.

What if I know my due date but not my last period?

Choose the due date method. The calculator will work backward to estimate your LMP, conception date, and current gestational age.

Why is there a compression level option?

The compression level adjusts interface density so the calculator remains readable and efficient across mobile, tablet, and desktop layouts.
