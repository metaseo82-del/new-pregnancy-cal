How the calculator works

A half birthday is typically six months after a person’s birth date. This calculator adds six months to each birthday year and finds the nearest previous and next half birthday relative to the selected reference date.

Compression level explained

The compression selector adjusts front-end presentation intensity. Low favors richer visuals, medium keeps a balanced experience, and high reduces decorative effects for faster rendering and lighter UI behavior.

Frequently asked questions

What is a half birthday?

A half birthday occurs approximately six months after your date of birth and is often used for milestone celebrations or school-related cutoffs.

Can I use this for babies and children?

Yes. This calculator is useful for parents tracking infant milestones, party planning, and social media posts celebrating a 6-month birthday.

Why does the compression selector exist?

It gives users control over the website experience. Higher compression reduces interface effects and prioritizes speed, especially helpful on lower-powered devices.

How do I add my AdSense ad IDs?
