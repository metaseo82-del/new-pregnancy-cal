How this Roth IRA calculator works

The calculator compounds your current balance at the selected expected return and adds annual contributions throughout the projection period until your retirement age. It also estimates inflation-adjusted purchasing power to help compare nominal and real outcomes.

What compression level means

Compression level changes front-end intensity. Low compression preserves more visual polish, medium keeps a balanced experience, and high compression reduces decorative effects and visual overhead to favor speed and lightweight rendering.

Frequently asked questions

What does this Roth IRA calculator estimate?

It estimates your future Roth IRA value based on starting balance, annual contributions, annual return, retirement horizon, compounding frequency, and inflation.

Does this calculator include taxes on withdrawals?

This tool focuses on Roth IRA growth assumptions and displays projected tax-free value conceptually. It does not replace personalized tax or retirement planning advice.

Why show inflation-adjusted value?

Inflation-adjusted value helps you understand what your future balance may be worth in today’s purchasing power, not just in nominal dollars.

Can I change compounding frequency?

Yes. The calculator supports daily, monthly, quarterly, and annual compounding to give a more flexible estimate.
